# 2022 Wytham Woods 3D model for DART

This repository hosts the 2022 Wytham Woods 3D-explicit scene for DART simulation.
The 2022 Wytham Woods 3D-explicit model (DART version) represents a one hectare model for a deciduous forest in 2022 in Wytham, UK. The model contains 529 individual trees, 520 trees inside the one-hectare scene were used. We matched the TLS data with traditional census data to determine the species of each individual tree and allocate species-specific radiometric properties. Full details of the model generation can be found in Calders et al. (2018), Liu et al. (2022) and Liu et al. (2024) (in submission).

Calders, K., Origo, N., Burt, A., Disney, M. Nightingale, J., Raumonen, P., Akerblom, M, Malhi, Y. and Lewis, P. (2018) Realistic Forest Stand Reconstruction from Terrestrial LiDAR for Radiative Transfer Modelling. Remote Sensing, 10(6):1-15
Liu, C., Calders, K., Meunier, F., Gastellu-Etchegorry, J. P., Nightingale, J., Disney, M., et al. (2022). Implications of 3D Forest Stand Reconstruction Methods for Radiative Transfer Modeling: A case study in the temperate deciduous forest. Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres, 127, e2021JD036175. https://doi.org/10.1029/2021JD036175
